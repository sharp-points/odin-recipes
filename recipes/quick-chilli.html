Quick Stovetop Chilli


  1. Place 1/2 pound of ground turkey/beef into large pot.
  2. Add 1 small chopped onion.
  3. Cook meat until browned.
  4. Add 1 can of chicken broth to pot.
  5. Add 1 large can of tomato sauce to pot.
  6. Add 3 teaspoons of garlic to pot.
  7. Add 1 teaspoon of cumin to pot.
  8. Add 1 tablespoon of chili powder to pot.
  9. Add 1/2 teaspoon of oregano to pot.
  10. Add 3 cans of red beans to pot.
  11. Add 1 can of black beans to pot.
  12. Add 1 can of pinto beans to pot.
  13. Add 1 can of corn to pot.
  14. Add 1 can of diced tomatos with chilis to pot.
  15. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  16. Mix and cook on medium-high for 35 minutes.
  17. Serve with sour cream and shredded cheese.
