Turkey Stroganoff


  1. Chop up 1 small box of mushrooms.
  2. Add 1 small chopped onion.
  3. Saute onions and mushrooms until onions are translucent.
  4. Remove mushrooms and onions from skillet, and add 1 pound of ground turkey.
  5. Cook ground turkey until browned.
  6. Add 3 teaspoons of garlic to turkey.
  7. Mix 1 tablespoon of flour with 2 cups of chicken broth.
  8. Add chicken broth mixture to turkey.
  9. Once broth/turkey mixture is simmering, add 1/2 teaspoon paprika and 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce.
  10. Cook for 3 minutes, then add mushrooms/onions to mix.
  11. Cook for 3 more minutes, then remove from heat.
  12. Add 1/2 cup of sour cream to mix.
  13. Serve over rice or egg noodles.
