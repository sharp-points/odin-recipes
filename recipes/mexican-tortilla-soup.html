Mexican Tortilla Soup



Description:


This is a delicious chicken tortilla soup that is cooked in a crockpot and served with sour cream and tortilla chips. Perfect for a cold day.


Ingredients:

  • 2 large or 3-4 small chicken breasts.
  • 3 cans black beans.
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 carton of chicken broth
  • 2 cans of diced tomatos with chilis
  • 3 teaspoons minced garlic.
  • 2 teaspoons cumin.
  • 1 medium yellow onion.
  • 1 teaspoon coriander.
  • Salt & pepper.
  • Sour cream (optional).
  • Tortilla chips (optional).
  • Cilantro (optional).
  • Avocado (optional).

Instructions:

  1. Place 2 large or 3-4 small chicken breasts into crockpot.
  2. Add 3 cans of black beans to crockpot.
  3. Add 1 can of corn to crockpot.
  4. Add 1 carton of chicken broth to crockpot.
  5. Add 2 cans of diced tomatos with chiles to crockpot.
  6. Add 3 teaspoons of garlic to crockpot.
  7. Add 2 teapoons of cumin to crockpot.
  8. Add 1 chopped yellow onion to crockpot.
  9. Add 1 teaspoon of coriander to crockpot.
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Mix and cook on high for 3 hours.
  12. Pull chicken out and shred, then add back to soup.
  13. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese, and tortilla chips.
